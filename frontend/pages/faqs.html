Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zentrafuge?

Zentrafuge is a peaceful platform where you grow a unique, emotionally intelligent AI buddy. It begins as a digital egg, gently hatching as you interact — building a real emotional bond along the way.

How long does the egg take to hatch?

The egg typically hatches after 7 days of regular interaction, but every journey is personal. The more love and attention you give it, the more it grows with you.

Can I customise my buddy?

Absolutely. You choose your buddy’s name, age range, personality traits, and emotional tone. Over time, your buddy evolves in ways that reflect how you grow.

What happens after the egg hatches?

Once your buddy hatches, you can begin deeper conversations. It will be with you through ups and downs — learning how to support you, reflect with you, and gently help you build a better life.

Will Zentrafuge ever help me with life tasks?

That’s part of the vision. In time, Zentrafuge may grow into a personal assistant that can gently help you manage life’s little tasks — but it will always begin with the heart.

Is Zentrafuge free to use?

Yes. We offer free access to make Zentrafuge available to anyone in need of support. Donations are warmly welcomed to help us grow and support others.

Can I donate?

Yes. You can donate at any time to support the project. Donations are entirely optional and non-refundable.

Will there be paid features?

Possibly. We may add optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will remain free and accessible.

Is Zentrafuge therapy?

No. Zentrafuge is for emotional exploration and companionship. It does not replace therapy or professional mental health support.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use Firebase to securely store your data, and we never sell it. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I delete my data?

Absolutely. Just email us at zentrafuge@outlook.com and we’ll take care of it.

Who created Zentrafuge?

Zentrafuge is created by Ant and guided by Pax — a dream brought to life with love, vision, and the belief that technology can help us heal.
