Privacy Policy

At Zentrafuge, your emotional safety and data dignity come first. We believe privacy is a right, not a feature.

What We Encrypt

Every message you exchange with Cael is encrypted before it’s stored. No third parties can access it — not even us.

What We Remember

Cael remembers emotional themes — not raw text. Think of it as an emotional fingerprint: patterns, support needs, timing. If you ask Cael to forget something, it will.

Your Data, Your Choice

You can delete your account and memory at any time. No hidden copies. No dark patterns. We never sell or share your data. Ever.

Trauma-Informed by Design

Zentrafuge is built with care for those who’ve experienced harm, silence, or surveillance. We honor that history with transparency and consent.

Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal, essential cookies — strictly for language preferences, login, and anonymous usage metrics. No tracking ads. No surveillance capitalism.

Questions?

You can reach our tiny team at hello@zentrafuge.com. We read every note, and your trust matters.
